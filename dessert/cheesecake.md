cheesecake (immersion circulator)
=================================

[this recipe](https://www.chefsteps.com/activities/the-quickest-simplest-way-to-make-bomb-cheesecake)
but without paywall and converted to (bad) freedom units

## preheat

heat bath to 176°F

## ingredients

- two 8oz packages of cream cheese
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1/3 tsp salt
- 3 eggs
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1/2 cup whipping cream (or buttermilk for more tart)


## directions

1. cream cheese, sugar, and salt first (blend until smooth)
1. eggs and vanilla next
1. finally buttermilk
1. (optional) strain through fine mesh
1. fill 8oz jars about 2/3 full (fingertip tight)
1. 90 minutes in bath

# graham cracker topping

approximately this ratio

- 8 graham cracker sheets
- 1/4 cup butter salted butter (melted)
- 2 tbsp sugar

1. preheat 325°F
1. crush
1. combine loosely
1. bake 15ish minutes stirring

# fruit topping variant

1. saucepan over low heat
1. add sugar to taste

# peanut butter cup variant

1. mix peanut butter, peanut oil, and powdered sugar until happy
1. drizzle peanut butter mixture into cheesecake before cooking
1. melt and mix milk chocolate and peanut oil -- create layer on top
